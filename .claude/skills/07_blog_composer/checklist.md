# Checklist

- Required sections are present.
- Section order matches `templates/chapter.md`.
- No duplicated explanation survives.
- Headings are nested correctly.
- Visuals are placed near the related text.
- 2–4 curated photos are embedded as figure blocks with attribution captions.
- The article reads like a lecture: every paragraph survives being read aloud.
- Paragraphs are presentation beats: at most 3 sentences each.
- Each concept section ends with a `{: .remember}` takeaway chip.
- Each section's key claim sits in one blockquote callout; bolding marks only viewer-catch words.
- At least three examples from the example bank appear in the body.
- The running example is the same in math and code.
- The navigation footer is the last thing in the file.
